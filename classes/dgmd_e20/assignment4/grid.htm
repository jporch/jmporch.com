Brandon Sanderson

Brandon is a best-selling and award-winning Fantasy and Science Fiction writer. He is best known for his "Cosmere" series: a collection of interconnected novels, novellas, and short stories set in numerous worlds and time periods, all of which are part of a giant shared-universe and contribute to a single developing meta-story.

Major components of Brandon's universe include the Mistborn trilogies and the Stormlight Archives. Other works like Elantris and Warbreaker offer standalone stories and cameos or other hidden connections to the broader world of the Cosmere.

"The Cosmere" is an in-universe name for the metaphysical model of the world in which Sanderson's stories take place. While much of them occur within the "Physical Realm," worlds similar to our own, the existence of the "Cognitive" and "Spiritual" realms dramatically influence the natural world, often in bizarre ways.

Additionally, Brandon is also known for his young adult fiction, notably the Reckoners series, the Alcatraz series, and his recently released novel Skyward.
