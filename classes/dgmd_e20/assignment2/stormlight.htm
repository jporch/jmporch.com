The Stormlight Archive

The Stormlight Archives is an ongoing decalogy of books that focus on the world of Roshar. It focuses on the reformation and development of the Knights Radiant, a historical group of warriors who opposed the Unmade and the Rayse, bearer of the Shard Odium, and sought to serve the people of Roshar.
