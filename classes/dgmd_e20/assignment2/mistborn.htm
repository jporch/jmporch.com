The Mistborn Trilogy

The Mistborn Trilogy is one of the core works from Branden Sanderson's Cosmere series of books. The term is actually ambiguous, because the Mistborn world involves more than one trilogy. Presently, there is the "original trilogy" which consists of 3 books: The Final Empire, The Well of Ascension, and The Hero of Ages. There are also three books in the "Wax and Wayne" series, but it is not technically a trilogy because it will eventually be concluded in a fourth book. Branden has also described plans for two more trilogies set in the world of Mistborn, but as of this time neither have been written. This page focuses only on the original (and currently only) trilogy.
